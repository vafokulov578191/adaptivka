Types of excursions

Bus tour

One of the most intense, affordable and popular types of recreation. During one trip, you can see the sights of several cities at once, and it will cost almost several times cheaper than a classic sightseeing tour with overnight stays in hotels.

Yachting

This is the same as a cruise on a liner or a walk on a ferry, only on a yacht there is more freedom, the size of the vessel is many times smaller, there is no annoying crew, you can feel like a captain on your own, stand at the helm. It is here that you will feel the greatest expanses of the Montenegrin Sea.

Jeeping

This is a trip on off-road vehicles with overcoming natural obstacles, this is an extreme sport and recreation, which is the passage of a tourist route off-road in cars.

Canyoning

This is an extreme sports type of recreation, which consists in overcoming canyons (flooded or not) with the help of climbing and other equipment, and sometimes wade, but without boats or other watercraft.
